# Anti-patterns

- no imitation of Japanese Gradients [For UI] branding, wording, assets, paywall, or black landing page
- no generic SaaS cards, rounded pills, glass panels, decorative grids, gradient-filled buttons, or dashboard metrics
- no fake historical claims or invented Japanese names
- no palette-driven recolouring of navigation and controls
- no tiny preview next to verbose explanation
- no modal for routine editing
- no hidden export state or dead controls
- no per-frame randomness, strobe, or unbounded canvas work
- no account, telemetry wall, or backend dependency
